About BEEWISE

Bees are essential pollinators for global food security, yet they are facing challenges such as Colony Collapse Disorder and pesticide exposure. Traditional beekeeping methods are labor-intensive and stressful for bees. To tackle these issues, we have developed an integrated beehive with wireless monitoring using IoT technology. This innovation aims to alleviate stress on bees and enhance productivity.
